Panes

Ogden First’s PANES program (O1|PANES) converts vacant storefronts into temporary galleries, transforming and revitalizing pedestrian thoroughfares into vibrant cultural hubs. PANES is a unique collaboration between local artists, social-service organizations, and schools.

We commission and curate art that has a connection to the space and neighborhood in order to connect organically with local audiences. Participating artists receive professional development and business training.

Alison Neville

Neville’s work varies widely including drawings on paper, dioramas, mixed media & polymer clay sculpture. She strives to understand more about the peculiarities of our world and sharing that with others.

Neville is a graduate of the Idaho State University. Neville has been a multimedia artist for eight years. She has previously shown her work at the Idaho Arts Festival and the Ogden Arts Festival. Her work can be found on her website.

Katie Strader

Strader’s process is exploration driven, utilizing atypical mediums, to achieve unusual texture and pattern. She delves into her most obsessive tendencies which are then translated into the worlds she creates.

Strader is a graduate of the University of Utah. Strader has been a multimedia artist for ten years and has shown her work at the Ogden Arts Festival. Her work can be found on her website.

About Us

Ogden First (O1ARTS) is a non-profit corporation established to create and deliver arts programming, in all forms, in the context of adaptive reuse of historic or iconic spaces, to create vibrant venues where artists can create, learn, perform and exhibit, amplified by our city’s architectural legacy.
