Walls

Ogden First's WALLS project is designed to unite artists and neighbors through a collaborative process and to create art that transforms public spaces. WALLS strives to be a community revitalizer and placemaker, with a diverse representation of local, national, and international artists. WALLS will showcase everything from old school graffiti to the latest techniques and technologies.

Our process empowers artists. We provide creative humans with the training and tools to seize their own future. We provide resources and opportunity, and they crate places for reflection and celebration.

WALLS will make Ogden neighborhoods more attractive, encourage community pride, provide employment for local artists, combat graffiti in neighborhoods, and engage young people in the beautification of their communities.

Current Projects

Literary Legacy

Coming August 26, 2019

Joe Schmoe

Literary Legacy is a mural on the north side of the main branch of the Weber County Library. When completed, the mural will feature the likenesses of famous Utah authors and pioneers.

Joe Schmoe is a graduate of the University of Utah. Joe has been a painter for 20 years. He has previously shown his work at the Utah Arts Festival and the Ogden Arts Festival. His work can be found on his website.

Junction City

Coming October 25, 2019

John Browning

Junction City is a mural that will be painted under the overpass on 24th Street and Wall Ave. The mural will be a tribute to the people who worked so hard on the rail lines putting Ogden on the map.

John Browning is a graduate of the Utah State University. John has been a sculptor and painter for 15 years. He has previously shown his work at the Utah Arts Festival and the Ogden Arts Festival. His work can be found on his website.

Upcoming Exhibit

Nuestra Ciudad

Coming February 21, 2019

Silvia DeSoto

Nuestra Ciudad will be a celebration of Ogden’s diversity. The mural will be located on the north side of Rosa’s Cafe on Binford Street and Washington Blvd.

Silvia DeSoto is a graduate of the UC Berkeley. Silvia has been a painter and multimedia artist for the past 30 years. She has previously shown her work at the California Arts Festival, the Utah Arts Festival, and the Idaho Arts Festival. Her work can be found on her website.

About Us

Ogden First (O1ARTS) is a non-profit corporation established to create and deliver arts programming, in all forms, in the context of adaptive reuse of historic or iconic spaces, to create vibrant venues where artists can create, learn, perform and exhibit, amplified by our city’s architectural legacy.
