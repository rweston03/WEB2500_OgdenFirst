Platforms

PLATFORMS has transformed a formerly vacant, disused lot at 25th and Adams and turned it into a vibrant, community-oriented performance and exhibition venue. This space will host sculpture, dance, photography, video, musical and literary performances on custom-built, 13′ x 13′ decks designed to offer a unique PLATFORM for local artists to mount their projects.

PLATFORMS is supported by RAMP. Weber County RAMP generously provided stanchions and chain link panels which will allow us to mount 2D exhibitions and support installations/weaving.

Current Exhibit

Wings of Spring

June 15, 2019 - July 31, 2019

Previous Next

Kensey Johnson

Wings of Spring is the work of Kensey Johnson. Kensey is a multimedia artist who specializes in capturing the beauty of nature by upcycling recyclable materials. Kensey hopes to provoke thought on the environmental impact human plastic waste has on the animals with whom we share the planet.

Kensey is a graduate of Weber State University. Kensey has previously shown her work at the Utah Arts Festival, the Ogden Arts Festival, and at the Mary Elizabeth Dee Shaw Gallery. Her work can be found on her website and on her Etsy store.

About Us

Ogden First (O1ARTS) is a non-profit corporation established to create and deliver arts programming, in all forms, in the context of adaptive reuse of historic or iconic spaces, to create vibrant venues where artists can create, learn, perform and exhibit, amplified by our city’s architectural legacy.
