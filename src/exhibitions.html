Kitsch Cordyceps and Portal

Alison Neville

Neville’s work varies widely including drawings on paper, dioramas, mixed media & polymer clay sculpture. She strives to understand more about the peculiarities of our world and sharing that with others.

Neville is a graduate of the Idaho State University. Neville has been a multimedia artist for eight years. She has previously shown her work at the Idaho Arts Festival and the Ogden Arts Festival. Her work can be found on her website.

Innerscapes

Previous Next

Katie Strader

Strader’s process is exploration driven, utilizing atypical mediums, to achieve unusual texture and pattern. She delves into her most obsessive tendencies which are then translated into the worlds she creates.

Strader is a graduate of the University of Utah. Strader has been a multimedia artist for ten years and has shown her work at the Ogden Arts Festival. Her work can be found on her website.

Wings of Spring

Previous Next

Kensey Johnson

Wings of Spring is the work of Kensey Johnson. Kensey is a multimedia artist who specializes in capturing the beauty of nature by upcycling recyclable materials. Kensey hopes to provoke thought on the environmental impact human plastic waste has on the animals with whom we share the planet.

Kensey is a graduate of Weber State University. Kensey has previously shown her work at the Utah Arts Festival, the Ogden Arts Festival, and at the Mary Elizabeth Dee Shaw Gallery. Her work can be found on her website and on her Etsy store.

Monarch in Moda

Previous Next

Jen Thager

Our first O1|WALLS project was a big one. Part of Ink Dwell’s international-scale #migratingmural initiative, #MonarchInModa took shape Aug - Sept 2018. A reception for the mural was held on September 22, 2018.

Jen Thager is a graduate of Weber State University. Thager has been a painter for ten years. She has previously shown her work at the Mary Elizabeth Dee Shaw Gallery, the Utah Arts Festival, and the Idaho Arts Festival. Her work can be found on her website.

Upcoming Exhibitions

Literary Legacy

Joe Schmoe

Literary Legacy is a mural on the north side of the main branch of the Weber County Library. When completed, the mural will feature the likenesses of famous Utah authors and pioneers.

Joe Schmoe is a graduate of the University of Utah. Joe has been a painter for 20 years. He has previously shown his work at the Utah Arts Festival and the Ogden Arts Festival. His work can be found on his website.

Junction City

John Browning

Junction City is a mural that will be painted under the overpass on 24th Street and Wall Ave. The mural will be a tribute to the people who worked so hard on the rail lines putting Ogden on the map.

John Browning is a graduate of the Utah State University. John has been a sculptor and painter for 15 years. He has previously shown his work at the Utah Arts Festival and the Ogden Arts Festival. His work can be found on his website.

About Us

Ogden First (O1ARTS) is a non-profit corporation established to create and deliver arts programming, in all forms, in the context of adaptive reuse of historic or iconic spaces, to create vibrant venues where artists can create, learn, perform and exhibit, amplified by our city’s architectural legacy.
